1. Introduction to Digivation (About Us)

Digivation is a strategic turnkey operations partner for major players within the Telecommunications Field Service and Infrastructure Maintenance industry. Employing in excess of 100 staff and with a well‑established footprint, we are ideally positioned for future growth and diversification. Our core business drivers are full site and tower preventative and corrective maintenance on MTN & Telkom. We aim to remain the first‑choice services partner by delivering above expectations and living our core values of Quality, Integrity, Teamwork and Accountability.

Why Choose Us

We operate in a fast‑changing Industry 4.0 environment where customers expect more than just a service. “Quality of Service” extends beyond technology to every facet of delivery. We provide turnkey infrastructure solutions that include all expected deliverables while minimizing business risk—backed by project management excellence across thousands of sites nationwide and abroad.

2. What We Do (Services & Specialisations)

Site Surveys & Audits
Coverage, capacity, security and redundancy‑focused surveys; troubleshooting; passive and active repair; detailed reporting for every test.
Radio Frequency (RF) — Design, Deploy/Build, Operate
TSS & planning surveys, structural engineering and design, azimuth verifications; civils, container reticulation, tower rigging, RF installations & upgrades, MW link installs & commissioning, optimization, lithium battery installs; FO/BO operations and preventative maintenance.
Point‑to‑Point & Point‑to‑Multipoint Connectivity
High‑reliability wireless links and star‑topology multipoint networks; frequency‑appropriate designs for Wi‑Fi, cellular, sensor networks and WAN/MAN applications.
Network Maintenance Services
Regular inspections; cleaning and painting; guy wire tension management; equipment maintenance; lighting & safety compliance; repairs and security enhancements.
Cloud Services
Tailored cloud strategy, implementation and optimization—ensuring a seamless, secure move aligned to business goals.
Decommissioning
Safe, efficient tower and equipment decommissioning with environmental responsibility and minimal operational disruption.
Internet of Things (IoT) Solutions
Partner‑friendly platform approach—wildlife conservation, building management, industrial/logistics and retail sensors; data collection, analysis, alerts and energy monitoring.
Savvy Solutions (Mobile Connectivity)
Flexible, business‑grade mobile plans from as little as R299/month; top‑tier products with dedicated support.
